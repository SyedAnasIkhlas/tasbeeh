About

Tasbeeh Counter is a free and simple app/web app which allows you to count/tally any activity you wish. Can be used for Tasbeeh (prayer) or just general counting like a tally counter. Tasbeeh counter is a digital version of tasbih / tasbeeh that helps Muslims dzikr activity. Project created by Syed Anas IKhlas.
